Nomination Categories

Problem-Solving Award

This award recognizes the connector who demonstrates an exceptional knack for solving complex problems and devising innovative solutions.

Going the Extra Mile Award

This award celebrates an connector who consistently goes above and beyond their duties to support their team and the company. The recipient may have taken on additional responsibilities without hesitation or volunteered their time to contribute to special projects.

Communication Award

This award recognizes an connector who excels in both verbal and written communication. The recipient may have a remarkable ability to clearly explain technical concepts to non-technical audiences or consistently produce well-structured and informative user manuals and documentation

Rookie of the Year Award

This award honors a new connector who has made significant contributions to the team or company. The recipient may have quickly mastered the necessary skills and become an invaluable member of the team or played a crucial role in a major project.

Technical Support Superstar

This award celebrates an connector who consistently goes above and beyond their duties to support their team and the company. The recipient may have taken on additional responsibilities without hesitation or volunteered their time to contribute to special projects.
